Additional Setting Elements to Consider

The Newspaper Format

Gaiman presents his story as if printed in a Victorian newspaper, complete with advertisements. How does this format create a sense of "normal life" hiding cosmic horror?

Baker Street

Both stories use 221B Baker Street‚Äîthe famous address. But one is a place of rational deduction; the other is a place of conspiracy. Same rooms, different meanings.

The Underground

Gaiman's narrator fears "the world-beneath-the-world" and won't travel by subway. Doyle's characters use trains casually. What does the Underground symbolize in each world?

Light Sources

Doyle uses daylight, lamps, and matches to reveal truth. In Gaiman, light reveals things better left unseen‚Äîgreen blood, alien rulers. When is light dangerous?

Tips for Parents

üíö How to Support Your Student

üéØ

üé®

  • Don't create slides for them

  • Don't stress perfection over authentic effort

The goal: Students should feel ownership of their pitch. Your role is to be a supportive audience and sounding board‚Äînot a co-creator.

üéØ Build Your Pitch

The Logline

One sentence that captures your concept. Format: "It's [Holmes element] meets [genre element]."

Example: "It's deductive reasoning meets space exploration‚ÄîHolmes solves murders on a generation ship where every suspect has been in cryosleep for 200 years."

The World

How does your genre change the setting? What's different about this version of "London"?

  • What does "Baker Street" become?
  • Who are the clients? The criminals?
  • What tools does Holmes use?

The Case

What mystery does Holmes solve? How does your genre make it unique?

  • What's the crime or puzzle?
  • What clues are genre-specific?
  • How does the solution use genre elements?

The Tension

What interesting conflicts arise from combining Holmes with your genre?

  • Does logic still work in this world?
  • What can't Holmes figure out using his usual methods?
  • How does the genre challenge or complement his abilities?
